Wesley Street

Hello! My name is Wesley, and I am a GIS nerd. I find LiDAR fascinating, and even own a copy of “Airborne Topographic Lidar Manual,” signed by the editor Michael Renslow. I got into GIS from a passion in remote sensing (LiDAR, HSI, and MSI), analysis, and beautiful maps. While retaining that passion, I have moved into managing and publishing data. I learned that without good basic data standards and delivery, the rest of it is very difficult to accomplish. I have earned my GISP, Professional Mapping Scientist – GIS, and GPC-GA II certifications.

When I am not at work or in school, I am spending time with my family, working on a car or a motorcycle, wood-working, or building something.
