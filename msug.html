Required Courses

Foundations of Homeland Defense and Security • Topics in Homeland Defense and Security • Multidisciplinary Approaches to Homeland Security • Strategic Planning and Organizational Imperatives in Homeland Security

What an incredible program that pushed me past anything I have done in my educational experience. I had always been very focused on the technical aspects of geospatial technologies, and before that chemistry. This class required reading a few hundred pages a week and and applying critical thinking to them. Writing assignements needed to be in APA format with citiations. It really developed my ability to put thoughts into writing.
